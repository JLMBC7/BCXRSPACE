Bakersfield College eXtended Reality makerSpace (BCXR Space)

The BCXR Space is a makerspace on the Panorama campus with access to a collaborative, hands-on environment where students, faculty, and staff bring their ideas to life.
Whether you're a beginner or an experienced maker, our makerspace offers the tools, resources, and community you need to explore, innovate, and succeed.
